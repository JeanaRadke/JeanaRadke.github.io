Macaroni and Cheese

  • Time: 20 Mins
  • Serves: 4 to 6
Ingredients Quantity
Ellbow Macaroni
Shredded Cheese
Milk
Butter		 2 Cups dry
2 Cups
1/4 Cup
2 tbsp

Step 1: Boil the macaroni in at least 1/2 gallon pot full most of the way with water. Add salt if desired. Cook time approx. 8 minutes. Strain (optional)

Step 2: Add the cooked macaroni back to the pot, add milk, cheese, and butter and mix until everything is melted and even.

Sammich

  • Time: 5 Mins
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Bread
Sliced Cheese
Sliced Turkey
butter/mayo		 4 slices
2 slices
6 slices
optional

Step 1: Toast the bread very lightly

Step 2: Spread buttor or mayo on inside of one slice for each sammich. Then stack a cheese slice, then stack the turkey slices. Finally place the remaining toasted bread on top. Secure with a toothpick.

Ranch Rolls

  • Time: 20 Mins
  • Serves: 6 to 8
Ingredients Quantity
Canned Bisquits
Butter
dry ranch mix		 1 can
2 sticks
1 single serving packet

Step 1: Cut bisquits into quarters. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

Step 2: Melt both sticks of butter in a round cake pan. Mix in the dry ranch mix until even.

Step 2: roll each piece of bisquit in the butter, all sides should be coated. Then place all pieces side by side, no spaces needed.

Step 1: Bake for 12 to 15 minutes or until golden brown on top.

Source: AllJeanasRecipes.com/RanchRolls
